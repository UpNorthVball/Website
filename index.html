Who Are We?

Up North Volleyball is a community-centered volleyball organization based in Seattle, Washington. Our mission is to offer engaging and competitive volleyball experiences while fostering meaningful connections. Recognizing that Seattle is a transient city, we are committed to creating an inclusive and welcoming environment where players can build community and friendships through the sport they love.

The league levels we primarily host are advertised as BB/BB+ as well as some BB- leagues that act as general pipelines to the more competitive leagues.

About the Founder: Dane Erosa is a local Seattleite and volleyball player who founded Up North Volleyball to create competitive yet inclusive leagues for the community.

Follow us on Facebook for announcements, or connect with Dane on LinkedIn (link available on request).

League Information

Downtown Seattle YMCA

$400 per team for a 5-week league

Each time slot is 1.5 hours per night, every team will play every week including finals.

Finals are bracketed into gold and silver to offer the most competitive games for the season finales.

Billings Middle School (North Seattle)

$500 per team for a 7-week Reverse 4's league

Includes a 1 hour 20 minute time slot every night, every team plays weekly including finals.

$570 per team for a 7-week Coed 6's league

Includes a 1 hour 20 minute time slot every night, every team plays weekly including finals.

Finals are bracketed into Gold, Silver, and Bronze to offer the most competitive season finales.
